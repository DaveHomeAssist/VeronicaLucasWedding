Is there parking at the venue?

Yes. On-site parking is available for about 55 vehicles, plus an overflow area. Handicapped-accessible spots are available. You can also take SEPTA Regional Rail to Wallingford Station. See Travel & Parking ↑

What's the weather like in late October?

The Philadelphia area in late October is usually crisp and beautiful — expect highs in the mid-50s to low 60s and cooler evenings. A light jacket or wrap is a great idea, especially for any outdoor moments near the sculpture garden. See Travel & Parking ↑

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We're working out the details with the photographer and videographer. We'll update this before invitations go out — but if you're asked to go unplugged during the ceremony, please respect the request. There will be plenty of photo ops at the reception.

Is the venue accessible?

Yes. Dixon Mansion is ADA-compliant with accessible parking, entrances, and restrooms. If you have specific accessibility needs, reach out and we'll make sure you're covered. See Travel & Parking ↑
